<p><p>The multi-band booster market encompasses devices designed to enhance cellular signals across varying frequency bands. Dual-band boosters operate on two frequency ranges, typically for 2G, 3G, or 4G services. Tri-band boosters support three bands, often covering a wider range of services, including LTE. Quad-band boosters extend coverage to four frequency bands for better compatibility. Five-band boosters offer the most extensive support, covering five frequency bands, ensuring optimal performance for diverse mobile networks and technologies, enhancing connectivity significantly.</p></p>
