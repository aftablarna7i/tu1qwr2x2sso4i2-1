<p><strong>The Full Dry Optical Cable Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Skeleton</li><li>Stranded</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Full Dry Optical Cable market is divided into two main types: Skeleton and Stranded. Skeleton cables feature a compact design with bundled optical fibers arranged in a single unit, providing strength and minimal bending. They are ideal for applications requiring high fiber counts in confined spaces. Stranded cables, on the other hand, consist of individual fiber strands organized in a loose configuration, allowing for greater flexibility and ease of installation in varied environments. Both types cater to specific operational needs.</p></p>
